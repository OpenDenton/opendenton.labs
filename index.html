Invest in the future of Denton

A smart city less about apps and more about creating efficient solutions to citizen needs.

As technology innovations drives forward, it becomes more and more part of our everyday lives. From simple smart phone apps to pay our electric bills, to autonomous cars driving us to the airport. Infrastructure, mobility, healthcare, and public safety are all industries being disrupted by technology - but how can we utilize new technologies to solve problems in our very own city. By working together to highlight challenges for citizens and the city, we're able to experiment and propose the technologies that work for Denton. By learning from the project outcomes of other smart city initiatives, we're able to provide benefits and minimize risks for the citizens of Denton.

What is Open Denton Labs?

The best government is an open one.

As a community driven initiative, Open Denton Labs represents a public private partnership that brings together hardware vendors, city officials, and nonprofits in the interest of designing and piloting smart city projects. While technology is a core component to developing a smart city, it is directionless without citizen input. We want to engage public / private entities with local residents to identify and fix the issues you value most - ultimately increasing the quality of living and making Denton a smarter, safer, more efficient city.
